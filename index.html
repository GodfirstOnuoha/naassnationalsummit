About the Summit

The summit is a National Association of Abia State Students National Summit 2023 of the 5th Assembly Senate Council which holds from 21st to 23rd December 2023 at Aba City, Abia State, Nigeria. The summit is going to feature the following programmes:

  • National Tech Summit
  • National Talent Hunt
  • National Business Summit
  • Joint Plenary
  • National Culinary Contest
  • National Praise Festival
  • National Football Tournament
